About Our Camps

Welcome to Chaukhamba Camp Stay, your ideal destination for unforgettable camping in Chopta. Nestled in the heart of Uttarakhand, our campsite offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by lush forests and stunning Himalayan peaks.

Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway or a longer mountain escape, our Chopta camp stay combines comfort, adventure, and nature at its best. From scenic treks to Tungnath and Chandrashila to cozy nights by the bonfire, we provide everything you need for a perfect experience.

We offer a variety of tent stays and campsites, including options near Baniyakund and Tungnath. Our tents are clean, spacious, and well-maintained, with proper bedding and hygienic facilities for a comfortable stay even in mountain weather conditions.

Guests can enjoy freshly prepared local cuisine along with popular Indian dishes. We provide budget-friendly packages suitable for families, groups, solo travelers, and adventure seekers looking for low-price camp stays or easy online booking options.

Known among travelers as one of the best camps in Chopta, we also offer group-friendly options and comfortable camps for every kind of explorer. Chaukhamba Camp Stay helps you reconnect with nature in the most authentic and memorable way.
